Top Map Bar List

World Flags and Body Bags

Open Full Map in Google Maps
  • 🇰🇷 (Korea) Dokebi Bar & Grill – 199 Grand St
    Lively Korean BBQ-bar slinging soju cocktails and street-food bites in a hip, pub-style space.
  • 🇨🇦 (Canada) Ontario Bar – 559 Grand St
    Canadian dive bar serving Moosehead, Labatt, and air hockey in a no-frills setting.
  • 🇮🇪 (Ireland) Iona Bar – 180 Grand St
    Classic Irish/Scottish pub with pints, whiskies, and live Celtic music in the back garden.
  • 🇫🇷 (France) Maison Premiere – 298 Bedford Ave
    Belle-Époque absinthe and oyster bar modeled on Paris cafés and New Orleans’ Old Absinthe House.
  • 🇹🇭 (Thailand) SEA Thai – 114 N 6th St
    Lotus-pond centerpiece, Thai basil martinis, and Bangkok-night-market energy in a walk-in-friendly lounge.
  • 🇲🇽 (Mexico) 320 Club – 320 Bedford Ave
    Tiny mezcal-forward speakeasy hidden past the burrito counter; great for agave flights.
  • 🇦🇺 (Australia) Isla & Co. – 107 N 12th St
    Casual Australian café-bar offering flat whites by day and Aussie wine spritzes by night.
  • 🇯🇲 (Jamaica) Kokomo – 65 Kent Ave
    Colorful island lounge serving jerk plates and rum punches to reggae and soca beats.
  • 🇩🇪 (Germany) Spritzenhaus 33 – 33 Nassau Ave
    Massive German beer hall with 25+ taps, foosball, and communal tables ideal for groups.
  • 🇮🇹 (Italy) Bar Americano – 180 Franklin St
    Intimate aperitivo bar pouring Negronis and Euro-leaning cocktails with classic Italian snacks.
  • 🇵🇱 (Poland) Karczma – 136 Greenpoint Ave
    Rustic Polish tavern with folk-costumed staff, Żywiec on draft, and hearty pierogi.
  • 🇬🇷 (Greece) Mr. Melo – 61 Withers St
    Vinyl-listening cocktail bar serving Greek meze and Hellenic-inspired drinks.
  • 🇧🇷 (Brazil) Beco – 45 Richardson St
    Cozy São Paulo-style botequim famed for caipirinhas, pão de queijo, and samba playlists.
  • 🇲🇦 (Morocco) Café Mogador – 133 Wythe Ave
    Lantern-lit café mixing mint-tea cocktails and Marrakech tagines for a mellow Maghreb nightcap.
  • 🇵🇪 (Peru) Llama Inn – 50 Withers St
    Modern Peruvian spot with rooftop pisco sours and shareable ceviche.
  • 🇯🇵 (Japan) Bozu – 296 Grand St
    Laid-back Tokyo-style izakaya pouring sake flights and sushi “bombs” (closing June 2025 — last chance visit!).
  • 🇸🇪 (Sweden) Tørst – 615 Manhattan Ave
    Scandinavian minimalist beer bar with Nordic imports and craft brews in a sleek, wood-accented space.
  • 🇺🇸 (USA) Desert 5 Spot – 94 Wythe Ave
    Western-themed honky-tonk with live country music, line dancing, and a mechanical bull in a Dolly Parton-style lounge.
